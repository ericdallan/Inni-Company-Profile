Welcome to INNi Accounting Program

INNi Accounting Program is designed for SMEs, CVs, BUMDes, and Cooperatives, offering affordable and user-friendly financial accounting solutions to streamline your business operations.

Key Features

Chart of Accounts

Classify financial accounts like assets, liabilities, and equity with ease.

Voucher Management

Create and track transaction vouchers, including discounts and payments.

General Ledger

Comprehensive record of all financial transactions for accurate reporting.

Accounts Receivable

Manage customer receivables with detailed tracking.

Accounts Payable

Track and manage debts to suppliers or other parties.

Data Export

Export data to PDF or Excel for further analysis with customizable reports.
